> straight_roads folder contains images of some straight roads with cars on it.
> straight_roadssegmented_images contains segmented images grayscale.
> straight_roadssegmented_images_colored contains segmented images coloured.
> contoured has images with contours around road and extreme left and right coordinate of road can also be seen.
> width.txt has width of road as last item of each list. first item gives the scene number, second gives extLeft, and third gives extRight coordinate of road in scene.
> straight_road_car has position of cars in scene with coordinates of 2D BBox, 3D bbox, and roty.
> road_width_instant has width of road and car corresponding to all cars present in the scene.
